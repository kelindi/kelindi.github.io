Overview:

Glitche – Minimal Personal / vCard / CV / Resume Template. Glitche is fully customizable and very flexible, 6 colors schemes. This template is perfect to promote yourself and your business, focused for: personal, portfolio, freelancer, photographer, artworks, art, artist, web designer, illustrators, designer, developer, programmer, coder.
